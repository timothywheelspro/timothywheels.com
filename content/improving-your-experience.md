## What We Heard and What We're Doing

| Area | What We Heard | What We're Doing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trust signals | Customers want stronger proof and reassurance | Rolling out verified reviews, clearer warranty highlights, and visible trust markers |
| Checkout | New buyers want less friction | Enabling guest checkout and simplifying the final steps |
| Mobile UX | Buttons and navigation feel small on phones | Mobile-first adjustments to improve tap targets and flow |
| Product discovery | Shoppers want faster comparison | Adding filters and clearer comparison cues across wheel types |
